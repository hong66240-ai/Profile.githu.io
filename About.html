Who we are

Built by people who bench-test
every batch by hand.

SIGNAL is a small team of switch obsessives — here is how we work.

We started SIGNAL because most keyboards are built to a spec sheet, not to a feel.

Every switch we ship is bench-tested by hand before it's approved for a batch — spring weight, bottom-out sound, wobble tolerance. If it doesn't feel right under a real finger, it doesn't ship.

Boards are assembled to order in small runs, gasket-mounted for a softer bottom-out, and tuned in-house rather than dropped in as stock components.
